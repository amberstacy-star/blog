Welcome to My Blog

Teaching, Learning, and Family Life


Hello and Welcome!

My name is Amber, and this blog is a space where I share my experiences, reflections, and lessons learned as a first-grade teacher, aspiring school librarian, wife, and mom. Each of these roles plays an important part in shaping who I am, and together they create a journey filled with learning, growth, challenges, and countless rewarding moments.


As a first-grade teacher, I have the privilege of helping young learners build the foundational skills that will support them throughout their educational journeys. Every day brings new opportunities to inspire curiosity, foster confidence, and celebrate my students' achievements. Through this blog, I will share classroom ideas, teaching strategies, literacy resources, and stories from the wonderful world of first grade and eventually the school library! In addition to teaching, I am currently pursuing my school librarian certification. My coursework has deepened my passion for literacy, information literacy, technology integration, and creating welcoming library spaces where all students can discover the joy of reading. I look forward to sharing insights from my studies, library projects, book recommendations, and the exciting process of becoming a future school librarian.


Beyond my professional life, I am a proud wife and mom. Family is at the heart of everything I do, and balancing career, graduate studies, and home life has taught me valuable lessons about perseverance, organization, and finding joy in the everyday moments. I hope to share honest reflections about navigating these responsibilities while continuing to pursue personal and professional goals.
