All About Me

Who I am

My name is Amber Stacy. I am 31 years old. I live in a small county in Eastern Kentucky. I have been married to my amazing husband for 9 years now. Together we have two beautiful daughters, Cali and Hazley. We also have a mini Bernedoodle, Remi. Together, our family is complete.



Work

I am currently a first-grade teacher. I have worked as a first-grade teacher for 4 years now. It truly has been a blessing, and I enjoy every second I have in the classroom.



Education

I attended the University of the Cumberlands to complete my Bachelor's and Master's Degrees. I completed my Bachelor's degree in Elementary Education with a minor in Special Education. I completed my Master's Degree in School Counseling. I am now attending EKU, where I am completing my Rank 1 in library media.



Hobbies

I enjoy many things when I am not working or completing coursework. I love reading. It is an escape from reality and keeps me sane. I have recently started loving puzzle books (Murdle, Murduko). I love anything that keeps me guessing and wanting to know more. I love fishing. While I enjoy all of these things, my children keep me busy with their sports. I wouldn't trade these days for anything!
